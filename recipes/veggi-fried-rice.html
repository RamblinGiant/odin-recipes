Veggi Fried Rice


Description

A simple, yet satisfying dinner!


Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 teaspoons + 2 tablespoons avocado oil or safflower oil, divided
  • 2 eggs, whisked together
  • 1 small white onion, finely chopped (about 1 cup)
  • 2 medium carrots, finely chopped (about 1/2 cup)
  • 2 cups additional veggies, cut into very small pieces for quick cooking
    (options include snow peas, asparagus, broccoli, cabbage, bell pepper,
    and/or fresh or frozen peasâ€”no need to thaw first)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon grated or finely minced fresh ginger
  • 2 large cloves garlic, pressed or minced
  • Pinch of red pepper flakes
  • 2 cups cooked rice (day old rice makes the dish extra crispy!)
  • 1 cup greens (optional), such as spinach, baby kale or tatsoi
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon reduced-sodium tamari or soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • Chili-garlic sauce or sriracha, for serving (optional)


Steps

  1. Heat large skillet over medium-high heat. Add 1 and 1/2 teaspoons of oil to pan once hot.
  2. Add scrambled eggs and cook until soft-scrambled. Remove eggs from pan and set aside.
  3. Return pan to heat and add 1 tablespoon of oil. Add onion and carrots, cooking until
    onions and translucent and carrots are tender.
  4. Add remaining veggies and salt. Cook veggies until crispy.
  5. Add rice to pan and season to taste. Letting the rice sit without stirring will create a
    crispier end result.
  6. Garnish with greens and optional sauces.
