Fried Chicken


Description

Get ready to always be the first person invited to any pot-luck or get together.
The only bad thing about this crispy fried chicken is how fast it runs out!


Ingredients

  • 6 chicken thighs
  • 6 chicken drumsticks
  • 3 cups buttermilk
  • 1/2 cut Buffalo Hot Sauce (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
Dredging Mixture
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 quart vegetable oil (for frying)


Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whish together buttermilk, hot sauce, salt, and pepper.
    Add in chicken pieces. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight.
  2. Heat oil in large skillet to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine the dredging mixture dry ingredients. Add chicken
    pieces one at a time to the dredging mixture. Coat each side evenly.
  4. Place chicken piece into the hot oil. Fry until chicken reaches an internal
    temperature of 165 degrees fahrenheit.
  5. Remove chicken from oil and place on cooling rack (or paper towels). Let chicken rest
    for 10 minutes before serving.
