Tacos


Description

A quick and easy recipe for ground beef tacos!


Ingredients

  • 1 pound Ground Beef
  • Tortillas (corn or flour)
  • 1 tablespoon Chili Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin
  • 3/4 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Ground Black Pepper
  • 1/2 cup Tomato Sauce
  • 1/4 cup Water
  • Optional toppings (shredded cheese, shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes,
    diced onion, sliced avocado, sour cream, guacamole)


Steps

  1. Heat skillet on medium-high heat and add ground beef (be sure to break it apart).
  2. Add chili powder, cumin, salt, oregano, garlic powder, and pepper to skillet.
  3. Cook beef until browned.
  4. Reduce heat to medium and add tomato sauce and water. Stir to combine and let simmer.
    Stir occasionally until most of the liquid has evaporated.
  5. Serve on warm tortillas.
